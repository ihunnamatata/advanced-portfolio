IT Asset Disposal and Destruction Policy

1. Purpose

This policy outlines the procedures for the secure disposal and destruction of IT assets to ensure data protection and compliance with regulatory requirements.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties involved in the handling, disposal, or destruction of hardware and digital storage devices.

3. Policy

All IT assets must be inventoried prior to disposal. Data must be securely wiped or physically destroyed in accordance with industry standards. Disposal must be documented with a Certificate of Destruction when applicable.

4. Responsibilities

The IT department is responsible for overseeing proper disposal practices. Staff must report outdated or decommissioned assets to IT for formal processing.

5. Compliance

Failure to follow this policy may result in disciplinary action and legal liability. Compliance will be monitored through periodic audits and reporting procedures.
