Incident Management Process

  1. 1. Detection and Recording: Identify and log the incident in a central system.
  2. 2. Classification and Initial Support: Prioritize the incident by impact and urgency, assign to appropriate team.
  3. 3. Investigation and Diagnosis: Perform root cause analysis and identify the potential resolution.
  4. 4. Resolution and Recovery: Implement the solution and restore the service to operational status.
  5. 5. Incident Closure: Validate the resolution, document findings, and close the ticket.
  6. 6. Post-Incident Review: Conduct PIR (Post-Incident Review) for major or repeated incidents.
  7. 7. Continuous Improvement: Log improvement opportunities and update knowledge base documentation.
